Proposal

• Project Name: Keke Palmer branding website.
• Overview:
For this project, I want to create an official website for the actress/singer Keke Palmer. This project will be a new concept. This project is important due to my interest in wanting to go in the field of designing websites for artists and working on their branding. It’s also important because me musicians and artist use websites in order to connect with fans and relay important information in regard to their projects/events. What’s included in the scope of work for the project would be I also personally used to work for Keke Palmer in the past; therefore, I’ve had experience designing things for her and what the intended audience could be.
• Audience
This project will be developed through deliverables in which will include design & prototyping, front-end website template. The intended audience for my project will be fans who already know who the artist is and those who are wanting to learn more about the artist.

UX Design & Prototyping – (Feb 19, 2020)
Front-End template Development with Bootstrap. (March 1, 2020)
